About Mobile Testing Summit China

The mobile testing conference held in China as the largest scale, most influential in its highest academic standard

It's launched by TesterHome, co-operating wth Alipay and eBay respectively, which has held three times of small-scaled mobile testing salon, one of the most authoritative domestic salons at present in the aspect of mobile wireless.


In this conference, we have invited famous mobile testing experts from home and abroad, including JLipps, one of the major developer of the most popular mobile testing framework: Appium.Through this conference, the whole level of mobile testing in China will be improved significantly and relationships with foreign open-source frameworks team will be closer.


The first Mobile Testing Summit China was held in Beijing on July 11th, 2015 successfully.
